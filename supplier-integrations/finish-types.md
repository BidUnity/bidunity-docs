# Finish Types

The following finish types are supported by BidUnity.

* **AAMA 2603 Paint**
* **AAMA 2604 Paint \(Liquid\)**
* **AAMA 2604 Paint \(Powder\)**
* **AAMA 2605 Paint**
* **Brushed**
* **Class 1 Anodized**
* **Class 2 Anodized**
* **Not Applicable** \(can also be represented as `null`\)
* **Oil Rubbed**
* **Polished**
* **Satin**
* **YKK Anodized Plus**
