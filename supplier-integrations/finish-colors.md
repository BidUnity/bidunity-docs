# Finish Colors

The following finish colors are supported:

* **Black**
* **Bronze**
* **Brushed Stainless**
* **Champagne**
* **Chrome**
* **Clear**
* **Dark Bronze**
* **Nickel**
* **Not Applicable** \(can also be represented as `null`\)
* **Paint** \(meant for custom paint colors\)
* **Polished Stainless**
* **Trulite White**
* **White**
